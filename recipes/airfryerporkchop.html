Finished Air Fryer Pork Chop

This recipe for the Best Damn Air Fryer Pork Chops is so juicy, tender and delicious, itâ€™s sure to quickly become a favorite air fryer recipe. With a few simple ingredients, and only 12 minutes cook time, these air fryer pork chops are perfect for a tasty dinner any day of the week.

ingredients

  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 (5-ounce) boneless pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the air fryer to 380 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, parsley, and pepper in a flat shallow dish; mix well.

  3. Step 3

    4. Coat each pork chop with olive oil. Dredge both sides of each chop in the Parmesan mixture and set on a plate.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place 2 chops in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes; flipping halfway through cook time. Transfer to a cutting board and let rest for 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining chops.

  5. Step 5

    6. Served hot and enjoy!
