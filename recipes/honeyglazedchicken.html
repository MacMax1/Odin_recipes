Honey Glazed Chicken

How To make Honey Glazed Chicken at your home

This honey chicken dish is easy to make with just 5 ingredients. I've used this recipe for years, and my family requests it often. Delicious served on steamed rice and garnished with sliced green onions.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces

    Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in bowl, set aside.

  3. Step 3

    4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    5. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.

  5. Step 5

    6. Serve hot and enjoy
